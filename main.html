BATTLESHIP

Each player will receive 5 ships:

1) Destroyer : 2 tiles
2) Cruiser : 3 tiles
3) Submarine : 3 tiles
4) Battleship : 4 tiles
5) Aircraft Carrier: 5 tiles

Where you place your ships is a secret to the enemy. So choose your location wisely! Players will take turns to attack each other.

Whomever sinks all 5 of opponents' ship - is the winner!

The computer's ships will be auto generated when you start the game.
Once you've done placing all 5 ships, Start the game!
